🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Social Media Manager respects your privacy. Here’s how we handle your data:

  • Email and password: Used only for authentication; stored securely in the database.
  • API keys: Stored in the database to enable posting; never shared with third parties.
  • Social tokens: Access and refresh tokens are stored only for the purpose of posting on your behalf.
  • No analytics: We do not use external analytics or tracking services.
  • Data deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which removes all your stored data.

We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.
